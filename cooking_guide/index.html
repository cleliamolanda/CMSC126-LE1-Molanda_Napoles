Pork Adobo Recipe

This is a classic Filipino dinner! Super easy to make and it's done in no time!

Cooking Instructions

Step 1: Marinate

Marinate pork belly in soy sauce and garlic powder for at least 20 minutes.

Step 2: Prep Pork

Coat pork belly in soy sauce and garlic powder while mincing garlic.

Step 3: Fry Pork

In a heated pan with oil, fry pork belly until golden brown on all sides.

Step 4: Sauté Garlic

Remove the pork, then add minced garlic to the pan. Do not burn.

Step 5: Add Spices

Add bay leaves and black peppercorns. Sauté for a minute to release flavors.

Step 6: Simmer

Return pork to the pan, add water until half-covered, and simmer for 30 minutes.

Step 7: Add Vinegar & Thicken

Add apple cider vinegar and sugar. Pour in cornstarch slurry to thicken sauce. Done!

Cooking Tips

  • Use pork belly for more flavor
  • Marinate overnight for best taste
  • Add coconut milk for a twist
  • Use banana leaves for serving
